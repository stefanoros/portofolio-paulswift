About me

Nice to see you!

I'm Paul, a game designer at Nintendo.

I studied game design at the NYU Game Center and now, I'm a game designer who lives in New York, USA and who's very passionate about creating unique artworks for video-games that can attract as many people as possible. I love to tell stories with my design and to create characters that every player will adore.

Since 2019 I started working at Nintendo but I also put my focus on the freelance part because I enjoy being "out there" in the world, being ready to come in hand is someone needs professional help.

I'm a big fan of books

I'm having a great time just reading things in my free time. I mostly look up after books that can give me inspiration. Sci-fi books always tend to sparkle something in my brain, to make my thoughts go wild, to put my imagination at work. I think reading is important in any form. I think a person who's trying to learn to like reading should start off reading about a topic they are interested in, or a person they are interested in.

I'm an animal lover

Animals are pure souls that only deserve love and protection. It doesn't matter if it's a horse, a zebra or a dog, the love I hold for animals doesn't have any limit. I see myself as a dog person, since I'm allergic to cats and currently I have a dog named Dudu. I like to believe I'm giving him the best life he could ever imagine. I love to spoil him, act like he is my little kid. Dudu loves long walks and that it's a big bonus for me too cause I enjoy jogging.

Loving photography

Taking pictures with my camera is one of my favourite hobbies. I love the fact that I can capture such wonderful moments in time. A thing that you see in my pictures is that I was not afraid to fall in love with these people... and by those people I mean my family. They are my everything, it means that no one gets left behind or forgotten.

I adore travelling

When I was working on my career, I was very aware of what I had done, what I wanted to do right next after reachiving my goals, and that was travelling. The world is full of wonders and after all, we only live once so I hope as long as I'm alive, I will get the chance to visit every single country of this world.

About the design:
For the main characters I took inspiration from the popular Tv cartoon show that everybody knows, and that is "Tom and Jerry". I wanted to make the main character: Jerry, as much human as possible and that's why I made him wearing bad-ass retro clothes. The big villains of the game are: the Kitties. I made them all look the same and even if they look adorable,like you want to pet them, like they are inoccent, the players will find out that things are not always what they seem...

About the design:
When I first started sketching for this game, I knew I had to add as much magic as possible, but also keep it close to the reality that we live in. Taking long walks into Central Park helped me to achieve a closer ideea to what I wanted the designs to look like and since the target audience for this game were kids between 7 and 12 years old, I choosed the animals in the game to have "baby" looks.
About the game:
It's like a bed-time story but put in a virtual game. Fairy-land is a mystic realm where every animal and magical beeing can find shelter in the fairy Bella's house. Everything is peacefull until one day, when the anti-hero comes and steals the book of spells from Bella, the good fairy. A hero must come in hand, or everything might be in danger...
